Public API

Who it's for Developers, startups, individuals — anyone with a credit card Mid-to-large organisations with significant or committed AI spend
Pricing Fixed, published rates — same for everyone Negotiated and confidential — typically 20–50% below list price
Discounts Batch processing (−50%) and prompt caching (−90%) only Volume discounts, committed-use agreements, multi-year deals
Contract None — stop anytime, no minimums Annual or multi-year commitment, guaranteed minimum spend
Cloud route Direct from provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Perplexity) Via AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud — apply existing enterprise agreements

When to use Prototyping, low-to-medium volume, variable workloads Production at scale (>$50k/year spend), compliance-sensitive use cases
📌
Procurement tip: If your organisation expects meaningful AI spend, contact each provider's enterprise sales team directly. Real corporate rates are never published. For organisations already on AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud enterprise agreements, accessing AI models through those platforms can unlock existing committed-spend discounts with no new contract needed.
